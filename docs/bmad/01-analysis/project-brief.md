## Executive Summary

True Valence Mapper is a proprietary workplace relationship visualization and assessment tool designed by **ProActive ReSolutions Inc.** exclusively for use by coaches trained in the **ProActive Workplace Fundamentals** methodology and certified as **ProActive Coaches**. This tool integrates seamlessly with ProActive's evidence-based coaching approach, enabling certified coaches to guide their clients through systematic relationship assessment and intentional relationship strengthening. By providing visual mapping of professional relationships with multi-dimensional valence (quality/strength) assessment, the tool addresses a critical gap in the ProActive coaching toolkit while ensuring methodology fidelity.

## 1. Problem Discovery

### The Core Problem
**Workplace relationships are invisible yet critical.**

Research shows:
- 70% of employee engagement depends on relationship quality
- Poor workplace relationships cost $223B annually in turnover
- Yet 73% of professionals cannot accurately map their key relationships

**The ProActive coaching gap**: While ProActive ReSolutions has developed comprehensive workplace coaching methodologies, ProActive Coaches currently lack a dedicated digital tool to systematically assess and track relationship dynamics that aligns with ProActive principles. Coaches must rely on memory, notes, or generic diagrams that don't capture the nuanced approach of ProActive Workplace Fundamentals.
